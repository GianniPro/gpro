Geography

Introduction To Geography

Geography is a coin of two Greek words “Geo”, representing earth and “Graphe” which means to describe. These two words put together make up the word geography which means the description of the earth, but it has more to do than just the description of the Earth. Geography includes explaining the features of the Earth’s surface as much as man knows, studying the natural phenomenon, human habitat, environmental resources and so on.

Therefore, geography is the scientific study of the earth’s surface, environment, atmosphere, and of human activity that affects the earth. Geography is also the study of the physical structure of the earth.

In summary, geography is the study of features found on the earth and the natural phenomenon occurring daily on the earth and how man uses the knowledge to solve his problem.

Importance of Geography as a Subject

  1. The study of geography helps one to understand what is going on in one’s immediate environment and beyond.
  2. Geography helps us to know where places are on the earth’s surface and to understand what is to say about the area.
  3. Geography helps in fighting climate change by knowing the causes and preventing it.

How does Starlink work

Starlink works through a beam of internet data, through the vacuum of space. Then, ground stations on Earth broadcast the signals to satellites in their orbit, which will come back to the user on Earth. Starlink achieves high internet speed by launching low Earth orbit (LEO) satellites this makes the internet speed faster and covers a wide area.
